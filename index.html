Option A · Bali Beach · 3 Nights
Sanur
Calm waters, local soul, unhurried days
✦Sheltered lagoon — flat, clear water, safe for swimming at any hour
✦Long beachfront promenade lined with warungs, cafés and local life
✦Fast boat to Gili Air departs directly from Sanur — no extra transfer needed
✦Authentic Bali feel — Hindu ceremonies, morning offerings, real neighbourhood
✦Quieter evenings — perfect wind-down between Ubud and Gili island life
Suggested Stay
Puri Santrian
Beachfront · Private pool bungalows · Garden villas
Traditional Balinese resort directly on Sanur beach. Lush tropical gardens, beachside restaurant, beautiful pools. The kind of place you never want to leave.
Also consider: Griya Santrian · Alaya Sanur · Tandjung Sari
Option B · Bali Beach · 3 Nights
Canggu
Energy, surf, beach clubs and brilliant food
✦The Lawn and La Brisa beach clubs — spectacular sunset spots for all ages
✦Surf lessons on Echo Beach — gentle beginner waves, excellent instructors
✦Best restaurant scene in Bali — Indonesian street food to wood-fired pizza
✦Rice paddy walks through the village — Bali's rural soul inside the buzz
✦More energetic — ideal if Laila wants activities, variety and things to discover
Suggested Stay
Desa Seni Village Resort
Boutique · Heritage pool villas · Rice paddy views
Stunning resort set in a working rice field. Antique Javanese joglo houses, beautiful pool, exceptional restaurant. Walking distance to beach and beach clubs.
Also consider: The Layar · Katamama · Canggu Club villas
